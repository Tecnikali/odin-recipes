Creamy Beef Tips with Egg Noodles

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds sirloin tips, cubed
  • ½ yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup (such as Campbellâ€™s)
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup red wine
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) package beef with onion soup mix
  • 2 (16 ounce) packages egg noodles

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Saute beef sirloin tips with onion in
    the hot skillet until beef is browned, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir cream of mushroom soup, milk, red wine, and beef with onion soup mix
    together in a bowl, pour into the skillet, and stir to coat beef in the soup mixture;
    bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, place a cover on the skillet, and cook until
    the beef tips are tender, about 2 hours.
  3. Reduce heat to lowest setting and continue cooking until the beef pulls apart
    easily with a fork, at least 4 hours more.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in the boiling
    water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 5
    minutes. Drain.
  5. Ladle beef tips mixture over egg noodles.
